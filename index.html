How To Play

It's Wordle, but the word grows two times.


You start with a three letter word, then go to a five letter word, then end with a seven letter word.


The letters from each word will carry over to the next.


If a word has two of the same letter, the following word(s) will not necessarily contain both!


Examples:

B

The letter B is not in the word.


E

The letter E is in the word, but not in the right place.


D

The letter D is in the word and in the right place.
